# Animal-Playing-Card Building the Prototype

Fighting Cock (Ga Choi)

The fighting cock is a sacred rooster and popular activity in Vietnam. The cock is also known as "Sacred Chicken" or "Combat Rooster." There are three types of fighting cocks:

  • Black Cock: Has a red comb and black neck for stamina
  • White Cock: Has ivory feet and yellow eyes for electric speed
  • Colored Cock: Has black, red, yellow, and brown feathers for flexability

During game time, the fighting arena is a 1.5m ring with a 20cm bamboo screen. The spectators stand around the ring while the owners' roosters are allowed in the ring for care. The rooster who loses will leave the ring twice without coming back.
